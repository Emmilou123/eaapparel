Emmilou Pangasian, the founder and CEO of EA Apparel, created the brand with a mission to help people feel confident and comfortable in their own skin. Growing up passionate about fashion, she faced the challenge of finding clothes that truly fit and felt right, which sparked her desire to make a difference.

With hard work and dedication, Emmilou built EA Apparel from the ground up, focusing on creating high-quality jeans and hoodies for everyday wear. For her, it’s not just about clothing—it’s about empowering individuals to express themselves through their style.

Today, Emmilou is proud to see so many people enjoying the brand. She believes that every outfit tells a story, and her goal is to make every customer feel unique and special. Through her work, she aims to inspire others to follow their dreams and embrace what makes them feel confident.
